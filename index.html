FILMi!

The original list of 100 films was created by an online discussion group comprised of film critics and other movie buffs that was first announced in mid-2004, and has now been updated through the year 2023. Every year since 2004, additional films were added to the list (and others were dropped), comprising approximately 100 more films that were deemed equally significant. These films are also noted - and highly recommended as well.
